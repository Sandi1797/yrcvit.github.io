Youth Red Cross - VIT

Best Student Club of the academic year 2017-18

Youth Red Cross (YRC) has been functioning in VIT since 1999 and is one of its biggest and most active clubs. It is essentially a social service club which aims at:

  • Raising awareness among college students about social evils like substance abuse,elder abuse and child labor;
  • Organizing social welfare activities in campus, such as health check-up camps and awareness seminars on health, hygiene and safety;
  • Helping the poor and needy, especially children, by donating blood, clothes, notebooks, and other useful items;and
  • Developing personality and instilling confidence in students by making them participate and organize events.

Youth Red Cross VIT strictly abides by the folowing principles of Red Cross:


  • Health

  • Service

  • Friendship
